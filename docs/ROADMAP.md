# DIOS Roadmap

Status: `PRE-ALPHA`

## Phase 1 — Foundations

- canonical source and revision model;
- object and relationship graph;
- quantity-record schema;
- measurement methods;
- evidence and review model;
- validation fixtures.

## Phase 2 — First vertical slice

Architectural room, wall and opening Aufmaß with structural cross-check:

- room and apartment topology;
- floor and ceiling areas;
- wall-segment graph;
- doors, windows and openings;
- section-derived heights;
- gross and net wall surfaces;
- marked-up review outputs.

## Phase 3 — Discipline packs

- Concrete/Reinforcement;
- Civil/Earthworks;
- MEP network and take-off;
- architectural finishes;
- drawing-to-ITP and drawing-to-RFI workflows.

## Phase 4 — Production readiness

- private deployment;
- user roles and approvals;
- audit and observability;
- performance benchmarks;
- backup and recovery;
- controlled exports and integrations.
